Dart Motion – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-10

Dart Motion (“we”, “our”, “the app”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and why.

Data we collect

  • Account data: email, user ID (Firebase Authentication).
  • Training/analysis data: your saved sessions and scoring inputs stored in Firebase Firestore under your account.
  • Camera access: camera is used to record/analyze motion (Live Coach / recording features). Video is processed for coaching features.

How we use data

  • Provide login and secure access to your account.
  • Save your sessions, analysis history, and scoring data.
  • Enable motion coaching features that require camera input.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We use Firebase (Google) to provide authentication and database storage.

Data retention

Your data is retained while your account is active, unless you request deletion.

Security

We use Firebase security rules and authentication to restrict access to your data.

Your choices

  • You can stop using camera features by denying camera permission.
  • You can request account/data deletion by contacting us.
