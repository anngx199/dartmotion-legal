Privacy Policy Dart Motion

This Privacy Policy explains how Dart Motion collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app.

1) Who we are

Dart Motion (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a darts coaching and training application. For privacy questions, contact admin@dartmotion.com.

2) Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address and authentication identifiers (used to sign in)
  • Basic profile details you provide (e.g., nickname, preferences)

Training & analysis data (user-generated)

  • Session records you create inside the app (e.g., scoring inputs, practice sessions, analysis summaries)

Camera / media (when you use coaching features)

  • The app may request camera permission to record or analyze your throws.
  • Video and images are processed to provide coaching features. Storage behavior depends on the feature you use.

Diagnostics

  • Basic crash or performance diagnostics may be collected to improve stability.

We do not collect your payment card details. Purchases are handled by Google Play / Apple App Store.

3) How we use information

  • Provide login, account access, and user profile functionality
  • Provide training/coaching features and session history
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations where required

4) Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app, such as:

  • Firebase / Google services for authentication and database storage
  • Google Play / Apple App Store for purchase processing

These providers process data under their own policies and industry-standard security practices.

5) Data retention

  • Account and session data are retained while your account is active.
  • You may request deletion at any time (see: Account deletion).
  • Store purchase records are retained by the store platform (Google Play / Apple) according to their policies.

6) Security

  • We use authentication and access controls to protect account data.
  • We use secure connections where applicable.
  • No system is 100% secure; please use a strong password and protect your device.

7) Your choices

  • Access and update your profile information inside the app (where available).
  • Request account deletion: Delete account instructions.

8) Children

Dart Motion is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact admin@dartmotion.com.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date below.
