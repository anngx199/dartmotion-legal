Account & Data Deletion Dart Motion

This page explains how to request deletion of your Dart Motion account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

Dart Motion currently supports deletion requests via email.

  • Email: admin@dartmotion.com
  • Subject: Delete my Dart Motion account
  • Include:
    • The email address you use to sign in
    • Your nickname (optional, helps us identify you)
    • If available: your user ID (UID) shown inside the app

We may ask for a brief confirmation to prevent unauthorized deletion.

What we delete

  • Your authentication account (Firebase Authentication)
  • Your profile data stored in our database (e.g., player profile)
  • Saved training and analysis sessions associated with your account (where applicable)

Important: Purchase and billing records are handled by Google Play / Apple App Store and are not stored as card or bank data by Dart Motion.

Processing time

  • Typical: within 7 business days
  • Maximum: up to 30 days in rare cases (e.g., fraud prevention / legal requirements)

Contact

For any questions about account deletion, contact admin@dartmotion.com.
